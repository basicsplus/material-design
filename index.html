Tools of the Trade

We offer knife sharpening for all types of "Wusthof Knives" including: Chef knives,Bread knives,Paring knives,Santoku Knives,Cleavers,Utility Knives,Tomato Knives,Carving Knives,Slicing Knives, and More.

Importance of a Sharp Knife

Keeping a sharp edge on your WUSTHOF knives is really important. Compensating for a dull blade by adding additional force can be dangerous. A sharp knife is a safer knife. There are multiple ways to keep your knives sharp.

Our Services

SHARPENING & REPAIR Having trouble sharpening your knives? Do your knives have chips in them? Do you need an extra-sharp edge for a special event? Bring in your knives to Basics Plus for a tune-up. We can sharpen and repair virtually any non-serrated knives, including hunting, and pocket knives. We sharpen all knives by hand using a variety of Japanese water stones. Prices vary from styles of knives and severity in damages. Traditional knives such as yanagi, usuba, and deba, require more labor and skill, thus will cost more to sharpen
